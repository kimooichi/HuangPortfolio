SOMERSET WARD

[INFOGRAPHIC | MEDIUM - CANVA]

This infographic was designed and created to display information in regards to the municipal ward of Ottawa, Ward 14 Somerset. The colour scheme references the primary colours found in the Ottawa logo, while the playful and welcoming design was intended to reach regular citizens and an audience with little to no knowledge on the ward. Overall, this was designed with the demographic in mind and keeps into consideration accesibility standards. This project was for my MPAD3002 civic engagement and public institutions course and received an A+ final grade.

[UI DESIGN, FRONT-END | MEDIUMS - HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, FIGMA]

Carleton Volleyball Club (CVBC) focuses on providing a welcoming space regardless of skill levels, access to high-quality coaching, training, and competition opportunities for their players and members. For this project, I was tasked with handling the UI design of the website and the front-end code with a team of three.

Stay up to date with Upcoming Events

Users can easily access news on current, past, and future events. Listing the date, time, location, event, and any additional information.

Organized Drop-In Bookings

Create an account and get exclusive access to drop-in dates and bookings! Users can view location, date, time, and the amount of spots left before booking their game.

Easy & efficient registration

Registration is easy! Whether you want to be a club-member, supporter, or a team player, online registration is a click away.

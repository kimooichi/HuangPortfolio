BLÉBLÉ CAFE

Bléblé cafe is a personal project that incapsulates my passion for tea at home — the name inspired by my childhood nickname. This project began with my hobby for tea making and sharing that with my friends whenever they come over. Each item in the menu are drinks that I've made up-to-date and served to my friends & family.
